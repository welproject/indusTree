PVC Curtains

Saving energy in industrial and commercial facilities has become an industry all its own. Of the products on the market designed to reduce energy consumption, our vinyl strip made into secondary doors and barriers is one of the most cost effective, efficient, and field proven methods available. Our vinyl strip curtains reduce the loss of heated or cooled air through an otherwise open door. It also improves comfort and safety, while protecting products and equipment in virtually any industry. Vinyl strip doors and barriers can be found on loading docks, interior plant passageways, freezers, and personnel doors.
