ACLRYLIC RODS

We proudly supply a vast selection of acrylic rods with varying degrees of tolerance to suit a wide range of applications. Acrylic, also known as plexiglass, is a tough and transparent plastic product that serves as an ideal alternative to standard glass. Acrylic rod is ten times stronger than standard glass yet half the weight. Many of our customers use clear plastic rods to produce signage, point-of-purchase (POP) displays, models, aquarium tanks, crafts, and more. Better yet, plastic rod is an economical choice that helps you get an excellent value without compromising on optical clarity, dimensional stability, tensile strength, thermal resistance, or other benefits of building with plexiglass products.
